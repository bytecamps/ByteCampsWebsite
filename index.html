About Us

Hi! We are high schoolers at Pinewood in Los Altos and we enjoy programming. We would love to teach this to kids (ages 8-15)!
We think that programming should be fun and strive to teach it that way.

NOTE: All camps/lessons will be offered online via Zoom.

Partners

iOS App Development

Target audience: kids 9-16 years old

Do you want to learn how to make your very own iOS apps using Apple's SwiftUI and Xcode?

We will teach you SwiftUI and Swift in order to make your very own apps in Xcode. You will also learn about programming fundamentals like variables, loops, if statements, functions, lists, etc. Throughout the private lessons (or camp), multiple apps will be built to show off! Examples include a dice app or even an interactive color game (with leaderboard) during the advanced lessons!

Famous companies such as Facebook, Instagram, or Uber use Swift too! By taking our lessons, you will be able to start building the app of your dreams!

Requirements:

  • A macbook w/ Xcode installed (instructions follow later)

Pricing for a private lesson: $45 per two hours

Pricing for a camp: $199 per week

Advanced iOS App Development

Target audience: everyone who has completed the iOS App Development Camp

During these lessons, you will learn how to make even more advanced apps using SwiftUI, Swift, and Xcode.

We will dive deeper into Swift and make a fun color game with leaderboard. For the leaderboard, we will even use the Firebase Database! We will also learn how to make more complex screens using SwiftUI.

Requirements:

  • A macbook w/ Xcode installed

Pricing for a private lesson: $60 per two hours ($75 dollars per two hours for two people)

Pricing for a camp: $250 per week

Games w/ Python

Target audience: kids 8-16 years old

Do you want to know how to program your own games with the app Python?

We will teach you what Python is and how to program with it! No programming experience is required.

You will learn about programming concepts like variables, loops, if statements, functions, lists, etc. Together we will make an array of engaging games that will be sure to draw attention!

Sample games include a space shooting game, a bomb clock game, a color game, and tic tac toe.

Requirements:

  • A computer

Pricing for a private lesson: $45 per two hours

Pricing for a camp: $199 per week

Procreate

Target audience: kids 8-14 years old

Do you want to know how to use the app Procreate to draw? During this camp you will learn the ins and outs of Procreate. You do not have to be a star at art to make fun projects with this app, all you need is these lessons!

You will learn how to navigate Procreate. You will also learn about different brushes, layers, symmetry, liquidfication (marbling), etc. It will be combined with fun projects!

Requirements:

  • An ipad
  • the app Procreate (around ten dollars)
  • Optional but highly recommended: Apple Pencil

Pricing for a private lesson: $45 per two hours

Pricing for a camp: $199 per week

Web Development

Target audience: kids 8-16 years old

Have you always wanted to build your own website?

We will teach you how to make your own websites using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. No programming experience is required!

You will learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript are and how to use them! You will also learn programming fundamentals and style your websites to make them look breathtaking. We will even dive into the framework Bootstrap! Multiple websites will be made throughout the week! After these lessons, you will be able to make your very own websites (and even publish them)!

Requirements:

  • A computer w/ Atom installed (instructions follow later)

Pricing for a private lesson: $45 per two hours

Pricing for a camp: $199 per week

Junior Coding Camp w/ Scratch

Target audience: kids 7-10 years old

Do you want to get started with Programming? During this camp you will be introduced to programming using Scratch.

We will use Scratch throughout the week and make some really nice (visual) projects! Students will be introduced to some of the fundamentals of programming while having tons of fun.

Requirements:

  • A computer
  • A Scratch account

Pricing for a camp: $199 per week

Note: private lessons are not offered for Junior Coding. If you have a younger child who is interested, please reach out to us and we can talk about it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer tutoring?

Yes! We offer tutoring. We offer tutoring for iOS App Development, Games w/ Python, Web Development, and Procreate. Just scroll up to learn more about what we offer and to schedule tutoring!

Do I need a computer/iPad? If so, what kind?

What device you need depends. For the Python or Web Development, any computer is fine (Windows, Apple, etc). For the iOS App Development camp, you will need a macbook (no windows computers). Lastly, for the Procreate camp, you need an iPad with the app Procreate installed.

How many students are in a camp?

We have a maximum of three of four students per instructor.
